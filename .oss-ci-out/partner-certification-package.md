# Partner certification package

_Generated from `docs/partners/partner-ecosystem-manifest.json`._

## Partner program overview

Structured enablement for consulting firms, system integrators, auditors, legal advisors, and resellers to deliver GovAI implementations with clear roles, commercial guardrails, and evidence-aligned delivery expectations.

Accreditation tracks that tie partner delivery quality to documented training, audit obligations, and renewal cadence without replacing customer-specific legal certification or altering GovAI runtime enforcement semantics.

## Certification levels

- **`audited`** — Audited assurance partner: Subject to periodic sample review or third-party attestation per playbook; may support regulated customers when customer legal and procurement requirements are met separately.
- **`certified`** — Certified delivery partner: Completed core training, passed competency checks, maintains evidence pack samples, and may lead implementations under documented support escalation.
- **`registered`** — Registered implementer: Completed onboarding, agrees to brand and disclosure rules, and can resell or refer with documented boundaries; no independent audit sign-off.

## Accreditation requirements

- Acknowledge audit obligations and renewal requirements in partner agreements.
- Complete training tracks aligned to target accreditation level.
- Maintain sample evidence pack and decision trace references per certification framework.
- Run partner_ecosystem_check diagnostics before customer-facing milestones.

## Training curriculum

- **`commercial-gtm`** — Commercial and GTM: Co-selling motions, revenue sharing documentation discipline, and partner-led discovery without over-claiming legal or regulatory outcomes.
- **`govai-fundamentals`** — GovAI fundamentals: Evidence model, verdict states, CI integration patterns, and customer-safe language for what GovAI does and does not certify.
- **`implementation-engineering`** — Implementation engineering: Audit service topology, environment readiness, export and report flows, and handoff artefacts for customer operations teams.

## Co-selling model

Co-selling pairs partner-led discovery and delivery with operator-provided product truth from the repository and docs; partners use the co-selling playbook for roles, disclosures, and escalation paths.

## Revenue sharing

Revenue sharing is documented at the relationship level in executed agreements; this repository describes categories and reporting hygiene only and does not define billing rates, ledger entries, or Stripe semantics.

## Audit obligations

- Do not represent GovAI outputs as standalone legal certification without customer counsel review.
- Follow audit-obligations.md for periodic attestations and incident disclosure boundaries.
- Retain delivery artefacts sufficient to reconstruct evidence timelines for sample engagements.

## Renewal requirements

- Commercial renewal of customer accounts remains separate from partner accreditation; see `docs/partners/renewal-requirements.md`.
- Partners must refresh training and disclosure acknowledgements on the cadence defined in executed agreements.
- Renewal should include a fresh `partner_ecosystem_check` diagnostic run and confirmation that audit sample obligations remain satisfied.
