## Manifest summary

Regulatory evidence manifest for GovAI: machine-readable index of EU AI Act–oriented documentation, validators, diagnostics, and export tooling. Operators use it to assemble technical files alongside legal counsel; it remains non-authoritative for statutory compliance.

## AI Act mapping scope

This manifest indexes GovAI repository artefacts that help operators assemble technical evidence narratives aligned with EU AI Act themes. It does not assert legal conformity, CE marking, or Notified Body outcomes.

### ai_act_annex_iv_technical_documentation_elements

- **Article:** Annex IV

Technical documentation shall contain the elements set out in Annex IV, where applicable to the AI system in question.

**GovAI mapping:**

- **Article:** Article 10
- **Title:** Data and data governance
- **Category:** data_governance
