# Evidentiary positioning (technical evidence packages)

GovAI is designed to produce **structured technical evidence**: append-only events, hash-chained ledgers, deterministic compliance summaries, and machine-readable exports. This supports **operator-run** conformity, risk, and oversight programmes.

## What GovAI provides

- **Traceable artefacts:** events, bundles, exports, digest manifests (see `docs/trust-model.md`, API reference).
- **Reproducible validators:** offline structural checks for interchange formats (`python/aigov_py/standards/`, `docs/standards/`).
- **Explicit versioning:** `policy_version`, schema evolution, release manifests.

## What GovAI does not provide

- **Legal certification** of compliance with any statute or regulation.
- **Court admissibility** determinations — admissibility is **jurisdiction-specific** and depends on procedural rules, authentication of records, and human testimony.
- **Regulatory approval** as a product feature.

## Manuscript-safe wording

Use formulations such as *“technical evidence suitable for inclusion in an operator-controlled dossier”* rather than *“legally binding proof of compliance.”*
