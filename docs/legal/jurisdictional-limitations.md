# Jurisdictional limitations

Governance and auditability requirements differ across jurisdictions (EU AI Act themes, sector regulators, civil procedure rules). GovAI documentation under `docs/regulatory/` provides **indicative** mappings for technical narratives; it is **not** statutory interpretation.

## Jurisdiction-specific factors

- **Admissibility standards** for digital records (authenticity, hearsay, business records doctrines).
- **Data localization** and cross-border transfer regimes.
- **Sector overlays** (health, finance, public sector) imposing additional logging or human-oversight duties.

## GovAI posture

- Ship **portable** interchange artefacts and validators.
- Avoid claiming **universal** legal outcomes.
- Encourage operators to attach **local legal review** to deployment-specific configurations.
