# GovAI Executive Summary

## Overview

GovAI is an open source and commercial platform for decision-level AI governance.

The platform verifies whether deployment and governance decisions are supported by complete, verifiable, and auditable evidence.

## Core Thesis

GovAI addresses the auditability gap: the mismatch between model-centric validation and decision-level accountability.

## Key Capabilities

- Evidence bundle verification
- Digest continuity
- Human approval gates
- Immutable audit records
- Tenant isolation
- Fail-closed VALID, INVALID, and BLOCKED semantics
- Governance-as-code roadmap

## Strategic Benefits

GovAI helps organizations:

- Increase auditability
- Strengthen accountability
- Reduce governance risk
- Operationalize AI controls
- Support regulated deployments

## Product Model

GovAI combines:

- Open source governance core
- Hosted compliance services
- Enterprise features
- Advisory and training
- Research and publication outputs

## Target Users

- AI engineering teams
- MLOps teams
- Security teams
- Compliance teams
- Risk and audit teams
- Researchers

## Long-Term Goal

GovAI aims to become the standard decision-level auditability layer for AI systems.

## Summary

GovAI turns governance requirements into enforceable technical controls and provides infrastructure for auditable and accountable AI deployment decisions.
