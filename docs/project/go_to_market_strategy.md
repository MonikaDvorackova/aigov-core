# GovAI Go-To-Market Strategy

## Purpose

This document defines the initial go-to-market strategy for GovAI.

GovAI combines an open source governance platform, hosted services, enterprise offerings, and advisory work. The purpose of this strategy is to convert technical credibility into adoption, pilot engagements, and recurring revenue.

## Core Positioning

GovAI is a decision-level AI governance platform that turns governance requirements into enforceable technical controls.

The central thesis is the auditability gap: model evaluation and standard CI checks do not guarantee that deployment decisions are supported by complete and auditable evidence.

## Target Customer Segments

Primary segments include:

- Regulated enterprises
- Financial institutions
- Healthcare organizations
- Insurance companies
- Public sector organizations
- Technology companies deploying high-impact AI systems

## Ideal Early Adopters

The most promising early adopters are organizations that:

- Deploy AI in regulated or high-risk settings
- Need stronger auditability
- Already have MLOps and CI/CD pipelines
- Face increasing governance requirements
- Value open source transparency

## Product Funnel

1. Discover the open source project
2. Run the local demo
3. Explore documentation and examples
4. Conduct a pilot evaluation
5. Adopt hosted services or enterprise offerings
6. Expand to additional teams and use cases

## Acquisition Channels

Recommended channels:

1. GitHub and open source visibility
2. LinkedIn technical writing
3. Conference presentations
4. Academic publications
5. Direct outreach to regulated enterprises
6. Partnerships with legal and governance advisors

## Commercial Offerings

### Open Source Core

Free and public governance engine and documentation.

### Hosted Service

Managed compliance gate and audit infrastructure.

### Enterprise Edition

Advanced deployment, access control, and support.

### Advisory and Training

Architecture workshops, governance design, and education.

## Pilot Offer

Recommended pilot structure:

- Two to six week technical evaluation
- Local or hosted deployment
- Integration into one representative workflow
- Success criteria defined in advance
- Final adoption recommendation

## Pricing Model

Revenue may come from:

- Hosted subscriptions
- Metered usage
- Enterprise contracts
- Professional services
- Training and certification

## 12-Month Objectives

1. Establish GovAI as a recognized open source governance project

## Summary

GovAI's go-to-market strategy uses open source credibility as the top of the funnel and converts adoption into pilots, enterprise deployments, and recurring revenue.
