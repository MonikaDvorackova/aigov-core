# GovAI Investor Narrative

## Purpose

This document defines the investment narrative for GovAI.

GovAI is building the decision-level auditability layer for AI systems. The platform turns governance requirements into enforceable technical controls and addresses the auditability gap between model-centric validation and real deployment accountability.

## Problem

Organizations deploying AI increasingly face regulatory, operational, and accountability requirements.

Existing tooling focuses on model training, evaluation, deployment, and observability, but typically does not verify whether deployment and governance decisions are supported by complete, auditable evidence.

This creates an auditability gap.

## Solution

GovAI provides a fail-closed governance platform that verifies evidence completeness, approvals, artifact continuity, and decision traceability.

The platform can block decisions that are unsupported, incomplete, or insufficiently documented.

## Why Now

Several trends make decision-level governance increasingly important:

- Rapid enterprise AI adoption
- Regulatory pressure
- Growing demand for technical controls
- Need for auditable deployment workflows
- Increased executive and board attention to AI risk

## Product Strategy

- Open source governance core
