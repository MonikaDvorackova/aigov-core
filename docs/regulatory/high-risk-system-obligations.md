# High-risk AI system obligations (orientation)

Title III, Chapter 2 of the EU AI Act sets **requirements for high-risk AI systems**. GovAI documentation and exports can support **technical evidence** that operators assemble under legal supervision; they do not replace statutory obligations.

## Themes covered in this repository

- **Risk management** — continuous identification and mitigation; see [risk-management-system.md](risk-management-system.md).
- **Data governance** — lineage and quality narratives; cross-linked from [evidence-obligations.md](evidence-obligations.md).
- **Technical documentation** — structure and export discipline; see [technical-documentation.md](technical-documentation.md).
- **Record-keeping and transparency** — audit logs and exports as **technical** artefacts (not legal disclosures by themselves).
- **Human oversight** — procedural patterns; see [human-oversight.md](human-oversight.md).
- **Accuracy, robustness, cybersecurity** — product security and trust documentation elsewhere in `docs/` remains the primary entry point for security posture.

## Non-claims

- GovAI **does not** classify your product as high-risk.
- GovAI **does not** perform conformity assessment or issue EU certificates.
- Runtime **VALID / INVALID / BLOCKED** verdicts are **governance** states for configured policies, not AI Act conformity conclusions.
