# Citation and attribution

## Software citation

Prefer the repository **`CITATION.cff`** at the repository root when your venue supports CFF. Update Zenodo or archive DOIs in [`../../research/citation-metadata.json`](../../research/citation-metadata.json) when you mint a persistent identifier for a specific release.

## Documentation and version pins

When citing behaviour, cite:

- **Repository URL** and **commit SHA** or **release tag**.
- **Paths** to the relevant Markdown or OpenAPI sources, not only secondary summaries.

## Fair attribution

- Credit upstream contributors and list the **license** (see repository `LICENSE` or equivalent).
- Do not imply certification or legal approval unless independently obtained.
