# Publication strategy

## Venues

GovAI-related work may appear in:

- **Systems and software engineering** venues emphasising reproducible tooling.
- **Governance, risk, and compliance** tracks that accept artefact-backed case studies.
- **Policy and socio-technical** forums when claims remain proportionate to evidence.

## Positioning

Lead with **decision-level auditability** and **evidence-bound verdicts**, then map to domain requirements (for example AI Act technical documentation workflows) without overstating certification.

## Disclosure checklist

- State whether evaluation used **local harnesses only** or **hosted** deployments.
- Link **reproduction materials** (commit SHA, commands, logs).
- Separate **engineering results** from **legal conclusions**.
