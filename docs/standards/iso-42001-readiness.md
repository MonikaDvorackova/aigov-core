# ISO/IEC 42001 readiness (alignment framing)

## Purpose

Describe how GovAI artefacts can support **AI management system (AIMS)** readiness work mapped to **ISO/IEC 42001** themes. This page is **indicative documentation only** — not certification, legal advice, or an attestation that your organization is ISO/IEC 42001 conformant.

## Readiness framing

GovAI helps teams produce **traceable, policy-enforced evidence** (append-only records, deterministic compliance projections, exportable packs) that reviewers can map to common AIMS control themes such as:

- governance roles and accountability for AI systems;
- risk management and monitoring hooks;
- documented decisions and change history;
- evidence suitable for internal audit preparation.

**Mapped** and **aligned** language means your operators connect GovAI outputs to your control matrix. GovAI does **not** issue ISO certificates, perform conformity assessment, or guarantee legal compliance.

## Relationship to other frameworks

The same evidence model can be discussed alongside EU AI Act oversight patterns and NIST AI RMF functions. Treat all framework references as **readiness support**, not automatic compliance.

## Validation

- `make iso-42001-readiness-check` — scans public landing and trust copy for unsupported certification claims.
- `make governance-standards-check` — validates interchange examples used in exports.

## Failure modes

- **Badge inflation** — marketing uses “ISO certified” or “fully compliant” without counsel. Mitigation: use “Aligned” / “readiness support” and link to this page.
- **Evidence substitution** — teams ship GovAI exports as if they were an ISO audit report. Mitigation: position exports as technical artefacts mapped by your compliance owners.
