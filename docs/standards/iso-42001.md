# ISO/IEC 42001 readiness support

GovAI provides **conservative, evidence-based readiness support** for teams aligning engineering artefacts with **ISO/IEC 42001:2023** (AI management system). This is **not** certification, legal compliance, or a substitute for an accredited conformity assessment.

## What readiness support means here

| Term | Meaning in GovAI |
|------|------------------|
| **Aligned / mapped** | A repository document or interchange artefact is **indexed** to an ISO/IEC 42001 clause or Annex A theme in [`iso-42001-clause-index.json`](iso-42001-clause-index.json). |
| **Readiness** | Machine-readable manifests and validators confirm that indexed paths exist and required checks are wired in CI. |
| **Not claimed** | Certification, legal sufficiency, control effectiveness in your production environment, or gap-free coverage of the standard. |

## Machine-readable artefacts

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| [`iso-42001-alignment-manifest.json`](iso-42001-alignment-manifest.json) | Canonical index of documents, themes, probes, and required checks |
| [`iso-42001-clause-index.json`](iso-42001-clause-index.json) | Indicative clause and Annex A rows with `evidence_paths` into this repository |

## Executable checks

From the repository root:

```bash
make iso-42001-manifest
make iso-42001-clause-index
make iso-42001-readiness-check
```

JSON diagnostics:

```bash
python3 scripts/validate_iso_42001_alignment_manifest.py --json
python3 scripts/validate_iso_42001_clause_index.py --json
```

## Relationship to other GovAI layers

- **Portable standards** (`docs/standards/`, `python/aigov_py/standards/`) — interchange validators and evaluation harness; useful as **documented information** inputs, separate from hosted audit verdicts.
- **Regulatory evidence** (`docs/regulatory/`) — EU AI Act–oriented mapping; complementary to ISO/IEC 42001 but not interchangeable.
- **Trust center** ([`../trust/trust-center.md`](../trust/trust-center.md)) — buyer-facing security narrative; see [`../trust/compliance-mapping.md`](../trust/compliance-mapping.md) for informative framework rows.

## Operator workflow

1. Run `make iso-42001-readiness-check` in CI or locally on a clean checkout.
2. Review [`iso-42001-clause-index.json`](iso-42001-clause-index.json) with your management system owner; fill organizational gaps outside the repository.
3. Attach GovAI audit exports and validated interchange JSON to your **management system evidence pack** where appropriate.
4. Record human sign-off per your CAB or risk process — GovAI documents gates in audit reports but does not issue ISO certificates.

## Non-goals

- No ISO/IEC 42001 certificate, mark, or registrar relationship is implied.
- No legal interpretation of ISO/IEC 42001 or national transpositions.
- No change to runtime enforcement, billing, ledger semantics, or `GET /compliance-summary` behaviour from this readiness layer.
