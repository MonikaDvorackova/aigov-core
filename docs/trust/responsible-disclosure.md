# Responsible disclosure

We welcome **private** reports of security vulnerabilities affecting GovAI. Public disclosure of unfixed issues harms users; please coordinate with maintainers first.

## How to report

1. Prefer **GitHub Security Advisories** on the canonical repository (private advisory flow).
2. If unavailable, email or DM paths published by the **maintainer** for the fork you deploy. Do not send exploit code to public issues.

## What to include

- Affected **version or commit**.
- **Reproduction** steps with minimal proof-of-concept where safe.
- **Impact** hypothesis (confidentiality, integrity, availability, tenant isolation).
- Whether the issue is **already exploited** in the wild (if known).

## What to expect

- Acknowledgement targets depend on maintainer capacity; enterprise deployments should contract **SLAs** separately.
- Coordinated disclosure timeline after a fix or mitigation is available.

## Safe harbor

Good-faith research that respects user data and does not degrade production systems is appreciated. Follow your counsel’s guidance on **computer misuse** laws in your region.

## Canonical policy

Root [SECURITY.md](../../SECURITY.md) remains the **normative** short policy; this page expands **process** for enterprise readers.
